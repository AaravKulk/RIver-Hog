Red River Hog


Potamochoerus porcus



The red river hog or bushpig (Potamochoerus porcus), is a wild member of the pig family living in Africa, with most of its distribution in the Guinean and Congolian forests. It is rarely seen away from rainforests, and generally prefers areas near rivers or swamps.
